FAQ

Most of the People Want To know more from us

As an interior design agency, we offer a wide range of services including residential design, commercial design, space planning, furniture selection, and home styling. We also provide custom solutions for architectural detailing, lighting, and landscape design.

Our design process begins with a thorough consultation to understand your needs and preferences. We then move to concept development, followed by detailed design, material selection, and finally implementation. Throughout the process, we ensure that each phase aligns with your vision and goals.

The duration of a typical interior design project varies depending on the size and complexity. On average, residential projects take about 2-4 months, while commercial projects may take 3-6 months or longer. We aim to deliver on time while ensuring the highest quality.

Yes, we can work within your budget! Our team is dedicated to finding creative solutions that fit your financial parameters. During the consultation, we discuss your budget in detail and explore various options to achieve your design goals without compromising quality.

Yes, we provide 3D renderings of our designs. This allows you to visualize the space and make informed decisions before any implementation begins. Our 3D models offer a realistic perspective of how your interior will look, helping to ensure satisfaction with the final result.

To get started, simply reach out to us via our contact form or give us a call. We will schedule an initial consultation to discuss your vision, needs, and budget. After understanding your project, we can proceed with a detailed proposal and timeline for execution.
